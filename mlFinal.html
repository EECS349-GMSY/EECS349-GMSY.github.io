Predicting Gym Congestion

A Northwestern University EECS 349 Project

The Problem

Our task is to predict the number of people at the gym based on attributes of the weather, along with date, time, and position in the school quarter. The task is important because it will allow students to better plan when to go to the gym, and predict if they will have access to machinery at the gym at certain times. This will save students time and allow them to have more efficient workouts.
